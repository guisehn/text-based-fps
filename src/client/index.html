Text-based FPS

Commands

  • set-name <new name>: Change name
  • join-room <room name>: Join room
  • room-list <room name>: View list of rooms
  • look: Show the room map and the enemies on your front
  • move <north/south/west/east>: Move to another place
  • turn <north/south/west/east/around>: Turn to another direction so you can view and fire your enemies
  • fire: Fire in your current direction
  • ammo: Show how much ammo you have
  • reload: Reload your gun
  • health: Show how much health you have
  • score: Show score table
  • respawn: Respawn if you're dead
  • leave-room: Leave the room
Just a node.js experiment by Guilherme Sehn. Source code available at GitHub. Based on a similar game by Eigen Lenk
